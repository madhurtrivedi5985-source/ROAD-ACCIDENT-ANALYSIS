## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze road accident and casualty trends.
- Identify high-risk areas and accident patterns.
- Examine the impact of vehicle types, road types, and weather conditions.
- Provide actionable insights to improve road safety and traffic management.

## 📋 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard includes:
- Total accidents and casualty statistics
- Monthly trend analysis
- Vehicle-wise casualty analysis
- Road type and road surface analysis
- Urban vs. Rural accident comparison
- Day vs. Night accident analysis
- Geographical distribution of accidents
- Interactive filters and slicers

This project presents an interactive Road Accident Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze and visualize road accident data. The dashboard transforms raw accident records into meaningful insights, enabling users to understand accident patterns, casualty trends, and the factors contributing to road accidents.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of total accidents, casualties, serious injuries, and fatal casualties, along with detailed analyses based on vehicle type, road type, weather conditions, road surface, urban vs. rural areas, and day vs. night accidents. It also includes a geographical map visualization to identify accident hotspots and regional trends.

🚀 Key Features

Interactive KPI cards for accident and casualty statistics.
Monthly trend analysis comparing current and previous year casualties.
Vehicle-wise and road-type casualty analysis.
Urban vs. Rural and Day vs. Night accident insights.
Dynamic filtering based on road surface and weather conditions.
Geographic visualization of accident locations using maps.
User-friendly and visually appealing dashboard design.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI
Power Query for data cleaning and transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for calculations and measures
Data Modeling
Interactive Data Visualization
